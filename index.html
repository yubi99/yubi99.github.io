Yong-Jin Han

Ph. D.

Kyungpook National Univeristy

I received my Ph.D degree (Computer Science) from Kyungpook National University (Daegu, Korea) in February 2016. During my graduate study, I did research with Prof. Seyoung Park and Prof. Seong-Bae Park on semantic web, machine learning, natural language processing, and computational social science technologies. The research is conducted as part of three main projects, CoreOnto, MobilePlatform, and EXOBRAIN.

Research Interests

Computational Social Science, Semantic Web, Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning
